  • Overview of the global trade industry and its opportunities.

Documentation Knowledge:

  • Step-by-step guidance on preparing essential documents like invoices, packing lists, bills of lading, certificates of origin, and more.

Advanced Course: Comprehensive Training in Import-Export Business

Introduction

The Advanced Course offered by Exim Global Trade and Traders is designed for individuals and businesses who already have a basic understanding of the import-export industry and are looking to gain in-depth, practical knowledge. This course focuses on advanced documentation, marketing strategies, market insights, and hands-on training in key areas like freight forwarding, customs handling, insurance, and packaging.

Course Highlights

Advanced Documentation:

  • Detailed understanding of all import-export documentation, including:
    • Letter of Credit (LC)
    • Bill of Lading (BL)
    • Commercial Invoice and Packing List
    • Certificate of Origin
    • Export-Import Licenses (IEC Code)
    • Bank Realization Certificate (BRC)
    • Shipping Bills and Customs Declarations
    • Step-by-step guidance on preparing and verifying documents to ensure compliance with international trade regulations.
    • Avoiding common errors in documentation that can lead to delays or penalties.

Marketing Strategies for Global Trade

  • Advanced techniques for identifying and targeting international markets.
  • Understanding buyer behavior and building long-term relationships with overseas clients
  • Strategies for promoting your products/services globally through:
    • Digital marketing (SEO, social media, and email campaigns).
    • Trade fairs and exhibitions.
    • B2B platforms like Alibaba, Indiamart, and ExportHub.
    • Pricing strategies and negotiation skills for international trade.

Market Insights and Analysis:

  • Tools and techniques for conducting market research and competitor analysis.
  • Understanding global trade trends, demand-supply dynamics, and emerging markets.
  • Analyzing trade agreements, tariffs, and non-tariff barriers.
  • Identifying profitable product categories and niche markets.

Practical Knowledge and Hands-On Training :

⦿ Freight Forwarding

  • Understanding the role of freight forwarders in international trade.
  • Modes of transportation: Air, sea, and land.
  • How to calculate freight costs and optimize shipping routes.
  • Managing logistics and supply chain operations.

⦿ Customs Handling (CHA)

  • Role of Customs House Agents (CHA) in import-export.
  • Customs clearance procedures and documentation.
  • Understanding HS codes, duties, and taxes.
  • How to handle customs inspections and resolve disputes.

⦿ Insurance

  • Types of insurance policies for international trade (marine, cargo, etc.).
  • How to select the right insurance coverage for your shipments.
  • Filing claims in case of damage or loss.

⦿ Packaging

  • Importance of proper packaging in international trade.
  • Types of packaging materials and methods for different products.
  • Labeling requirements and compliance with international standards.
  • Ensuring product safety during transit.
Who Should Enroll?
  • Business owners and entrepreneurs looking to expand their operations globally.
  • Professionals working in logistics, supply chain, or international trade.
  • Individuals with basic knowledge of import-export who want to gain advanced, practical skills.
Course Benefits
  • Master the end-to-end process of import-export operations.
  • Gain practical, hands-on experience in key areas like freight forwarding, customs handling, and packaging.

Export Business Consultancy Services

At Exim Global Trade and Traders, we provide end-to-end consultancy services to help you establish and grow your own export business. From setting up your business, handling all documentation and compliance, to creating a strong global presence through marketing and social media, we are your one-stop solution for all your export business needs.

Our Consultancy Services

  • Business Setup and Registration : Assistance with business registration, obtaining IEC, choosing the right structure, and opening an account for global trade.
  • Documentation and Compliance: Comprehensive support for export documentation, compliance, customs clearance, and licenses—covering invoices, BL, LC, certificates, and regulatory requirements for smooth global trade.
  • CA and Legal Services: Chartered Accountant (CA) Services, Legal Services
  • Legal Services: Drafting/reviewing contracts, IPR assistance, and resolving legal issues to protect your business and ensure smooth international trade operations.
  • Marketing and Branding Services: Digital Marketing, Business Media Platforms, Branding
  • Global Market Presence: Market research, global trade insights, and strategies to enter new markets—plus networking with buyers, suppliers, and trade organizations to boost your international presence.
